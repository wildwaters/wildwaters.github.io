U of T Outdoors

Wild Waters has been awarded an ATLAS grant to enable a pilot project for the U of T Outdoors initiative. Students will have the opportunity to participate in immersive experiential education modules, including two weekend canoe trips in the Fall semester, and a canoe-building project in the Spring. U of T Outdoors seeks to expand opportunities for students to learn through intensive experiences outside the clasroom, often involving elements of physical hardship and emotional challenges.
